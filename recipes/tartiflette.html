Tartiflette

Description:

This wonderful Tartiflette is waiting for you in the mountains of France, the Alpes, where you can enjoy this meal made essentially of cheese after a good day of ski...

Ingredients:

  • Roblochon cheese
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Lardons (bacon like meat)

Steps:

  1. Do your potatoes a favor: get them rid of their skin
  2. Chop the onions
  3. Cook the potatoes, the onions and the lardons in a frying pan
  4. Put everything in a big oven dish
  5. Cut the cheese in half and put it on top
  6. Roast it 30 min in the oven
  7. Eat until you can't eat more
