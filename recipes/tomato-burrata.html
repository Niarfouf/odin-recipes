Cherry Tomatoes and Burrata

Description:

As a starter these cooked cherry tomatoes with burrata on a grilled bread will make you eat more than you would think of! Take one more!

Ingredients:

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Burrata cheese
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Bread

Steps:

  1. Slowly cook the tomatoes with olive oil in the oven
  2. Cut the bread in slices, rub them with garlic and cook them a little in the oven, not too much!
  3. Put a portion of tomato on each slice of bread
  4. Put a small portion of burrata cheese on top of it
  5. Add a basil leaf
  6. Ready to taste
