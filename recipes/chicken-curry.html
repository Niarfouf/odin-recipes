Chicken Coconut Curry

Description:

This chicken coconut curry with rice is more than enough to make you have a good meal, if you like spices !

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Coconut milk
  • Curry spices
  • Onions
  • Coriander
  • Rice

Steps:

  1. Cut the onions and the chicken in small pieces
  2. Cook the chicken in a pan with oil, and start boiling water for the rice
  3. After 6 minutes add the onions and the spices
  4. After 5 more minutes add the coconut milk, add the rice in the water
  5. Cook until you think it's cooked
  6. Serve the chicken on top of the rice with coriander leaves
